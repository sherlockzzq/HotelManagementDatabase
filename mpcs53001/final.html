About Our Management System

Our hotel management system is aimed to provide you better service for checking hotel and customer information. Users can check information on hotel, customer record, room reservation and employee.

The employee standard information includes his name, gender, birthday, ID number, and their duty to provide services to customer. The customers' records stored in the database contain their indentity, address, check in time and check out time, etc. By room catagory, each room has different design, prices and facility status. Also order status like reservation are also included.

To operate our hotel management database, you should try Hotel, Client, Employee parts. Search Info will lead you to project8.
